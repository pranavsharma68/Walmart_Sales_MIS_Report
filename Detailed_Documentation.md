# Walmart Sales MIS Reporting Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a comprehensive MIS (Management Information System) reporting solution using Walmart sales data. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into structured reports and an interactive dashboard that enables business users to monitor sales performance across different reporting periods, stores, departments, and holiday seasons.

The project demonstrates how regular MIS reporting can support management in tracking KPIs, identifying trends, and making informed business decisions.

---

# Business Problem

Business stakeholders required a centralized reporting solution to:

- Monitor sales performance at weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly levels
- Compare performance across different stores and departments
- Evaluate sales during holiday periods
- Track overall business KPIs through a single dashboard
- Reduce manual reporting effort and improve reporting accuracy

The goal was to automate reporting and provide decision-makers with timely and reliable business insights.

---

# Dataset Description

The project uses Walmart sales data consisting of:

- Weekly sales records
- Store information
- Department information
- Holiday indicators
- Temperature
- Fuel Price
- CPI (Consumer Price Index)
- Unemployment Rate
- Store Type and Size

The raw data was cleaned, transformed, and structured before creating MIS reports and dashboard visualizations.

# Key Insights

- Weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly reports provide continuous visibility into business performance.

- Store-level analysis highlights high-performing and underperforming stores, enabling targeted operational improvements.

- Department-level reporting identifies departments contributing the highest sales and those requiring performance improvement.

- Holiday analysis reveals noticeable variations in sales during holiday periods, supporting demand forecasting and promotional planning.

- Consolidated KPI reporting enables management to monitor overall business performance from a single interactive dashboard.
