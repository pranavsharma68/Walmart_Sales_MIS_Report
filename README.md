## Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a comprehensive Management Information System (MIS) reporting solution using Walmart sales data. The solution transforms raw transactional data into structured reports and an interactive dashboard, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

The analysis focuses on identifying:

- Sales performance across multiple reporting periods
- Store-wise business performance
- Department-wise sales trends
- Holiday impact on sales
- Key business performance indicators (KPIs)

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- MIS Reporting

---

## Key Insights

- Developed weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly MIS reports for continuous business monitoring
- Compared store and department performance to identify high- and low-performing areas
- Analyzed holiday sales trends to support business planning and promotional strategies
- Consolidated multiple business reports into a centralized executive dashboard
